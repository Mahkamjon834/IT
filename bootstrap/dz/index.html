The NEED


The health system
isn't equitable for
diverse patients.



Medical racism. Severe distrust in medical providers.
Misdiagnoses. Lack of access to coverage. These are just
a few barriers culturally diverse patients face. We believe
every human deserves equitable health care. That's why
we built Violet.

How we do it



Violet is the first to
standardize cultural

improvement.
